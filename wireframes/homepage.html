Wireframe Notes

Header

Simple navigation. Should stick to the top on scroll in the prototype.

Hero Section

Must have a very strong, attention-grabbing headline. The primary CTA should be highly visible.

Modules Section

A grid layout works well. In the prototype, these should be interactive, possibly an accordion that reveals more details on click.

Testimonials

Card-based layout. Should include a quote, name, and social handle to feel authentic.

Pricing Section

Keep it simple. One main tier to avoid decision fatigue. Reiterate key benefits and include the final CTA.

Footer

Standard footer content. Privacy Policy, ToS, social media links.
